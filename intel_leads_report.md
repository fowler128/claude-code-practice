## D) Red Flags

1. Existing enterprise legal panel with low willingness to add boutique counsel.
2. ITAR/defense constraints reducing practical foreign national placement in key roles.
3. Blanket “no sponsorship” hiring policy across core roles.
4. Weak corroboration (press hype without hiring or expansion evidence).
5. Staffing-arbitrage pattern versus true employer-side workforce planning.
