## D) Red Flags / Poor-Fit Indicators (even when logos look attractive)

1. **Heavy legal panel procurement already in place** (common for larger enterprises like Crusoe/EquipmentShare).
2. **Defense/ITAR-heavy workflows** may reduce foreign national hiring flexibility in some teams (not all teams).
3. **Company policy explicitly “no sponsorship” across most roles** can limit near-term visa work, though compliance advisory may remain relevant.
4. **Too-small startups (<10 employees) without clear funding or expansion** should be deprioritized.
5. **Staffing-body-shop patterns** should be excluded if model appears arbitrage-driven rather than true employer-side talent strategy.
6. **Signals based only on vanity press with no hiring/expansion corroboration** should be downgraded.
