## 3) Strongest business reason to contact them now

- Many targets are at **post-funding, pre-friction** inflection points where hiring speed can break delivery plans.
- Several leads show **new facilities / expansion / commercialization events**, increasing exposure to I-9 and worksite risk.
- Cross-border operations and foreign-market ties suggest immediate need for **TN/L-1/H-1B planning**, not ad hoc filing.

---

## 4) Red flags

- Defense/ITAR constraints may narrow practical use cases in some teams (especially Saronic).
- Large raises can coincide with enterprise legal panel lock-in (higher procurement friction).
- If roles are broadly marked “no sponsorship,” prioritize compliance advisory entry instead of sponsorship-first pitch.
- For very early-stage teams, budget/process maturity may lag despite strong headline funding.
