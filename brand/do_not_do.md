# Do Not Do - Universal Brand Guidelines

## Overview
This document outlines practices, language, and approaches to AVOID across all brands and projects. These are universal restrictions that apply regardless of the specific brand voice.

## Content Restrictions

### Never Include
- ❌ Misleading or false claims
- ❌ Unsubstantiated guarantees ("100% guaranteed results")
- ❌ Comparisons that demean competitors by name
- ❌ Confidential client information without permission
- ❌ Proprietary data or trade secrets
- ❌ Personal contact information (unless explicitly authorized)
- ❌ Pricing without proper approval
- ❌ Legal advice or medical advice
- ❌ Political or divisive social commentary

### Prohibited Language Patterns
- ❌ Absolute terms without evidence: "always", "never", "guaranteed", "perfect"
- ❌ Manipulative urgency: "Act now or lose out forever!"
- ❌ Clickbait headlines: "You won't believe...", "This one trick..."
- ❌ Excessive punctuation: "Amazing!!!", "Really???"
- ❌ All caps for emphasis (except acronyms)
- ❌ Unprofessional slang or profanity
- ❌ Cultural stereotypes or insensitive language

## Communication Practices to Avoid

### Don't:
1. **Overpromise and Underdeliver**
   - Set realistic expectations
   - Be honest about limitations
   - Acknowledge when something is uncertain

2. **Use Fear-Based Marketing**
   - No scare tactics
   - Don't prey on insecurities
   - Focus on positive outcomes, not negative consequences

3. **Ignore Data Privacy**
   - Don't request unnecessary personal information
   - Respect GDPR and privacy regulations
   - Never share client data without consent

4. **Spam or Over-Communicate**
   - Respect communication frequency preferences
   - Provide opt-out options
   - Don't send unsolicited bulk messages

5. **Copy Competitors Directly**
   - Draw inspiration, don't plagiarize
   - Develop unique value propositions
   - Build original content

6. **Make Technical Assumptions**
   - Don't assume everyone understands jargon
   - Explain acronyms on first use
   - Provide context for technical concepts

7. **Neglect Accessibility**
   - Don't use images without alt text
   - Avoid color-only communication
   - Ensure readable font sizes and contrast

8. **Rush Quality for Speed**
   - Don't sacrifice accuracy for quick turnaround
   - Proofread before publishing
   - Test functionality before launch

## Ethical Guidelines

### Maintain Integrity
- Don't misrepresent capabilities or experience
- Don't fabricate testimonials or case studies
- Don't hide negative information that clients should know
- Don't engage in bait-and-switch tactics

### Respect Boundaries
- Don't contact people outside business hours without permission
- Don't pressure for immediate decisions
- Don't use guilt or manipulation tactics
- Don't ignore "no" or withdrawal of consent

### Protect Intellectual Property
- Don't use copyrighted images without license
- Don't copy competitor content verbatim
- Don't violate trademark rights
- Don't share proprietary methodologies without authorization

## Platform-Specific Restrictions

### LinkedIn
- Don't send generic connection requests with sales pitches
- Don't post more than 2-3 times per day
- Don't use automation that violates LinkedIn ToS
- Don't tag people without relevance

### Email
- Don't hide unsubscribe options
- Don't use deceptive subject lines
- Don't send from no-reply addresses without support alternative
- Don't purchase email lists

### Social Media
- Don't delete negative (but legitimate) comments
- Don't engage in comment wars
- Don't post during sensitive events without care
- Don't use trending hashtags inappropriately

## Red Flags - Stop and Review

If content includes any of these, pause and consult:
- Legal claims or advice
- Financial predictions or investment advice
- Health or medical claims
- Comparisons to named competitors
- Client-specific confidential information
- Controversial political or social positions
- Pricing commitments
- Service guarantees without terms

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:
- ✅ All claims are truthful and substantiated
- ✅ Tone aligns with brand voice
- ✅ No prohibited language or practices
- ✅ Grammar and spelling are correct
- ✅ Links and images work properly
- ✅ Accessible to all audiences
- ✅ Complies with platform guidelines
- ✅ Reviewed by appropriate stakeholders

---

*When in doubt, ask. It's better to delay publication than to damage brand reputation.*
