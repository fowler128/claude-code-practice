# LinkedIn Post Factory

**Use Case**: When you need to create high-quality LinkedIn posts that drive engagement, build authority, and generate leads - plus repurpose that content across other platforms.

## Post Anatomy Deep Dive

### The Hook (First 1-2 Lines)
**Purpose**: Stop the scroll, create curiosity, promise value

**Types:**
1. **Stat Hook**: "We analyzed 1,247 leads and found 83% fail this simple test."
2. **Story Hook**: "Three months ago, our client's lead conversion rate was 4%. Yesterday it hit 17%."
3. **Question Hook**: "Why do most B2B companies waste 60% of their marketing budget?"
4. **Contrarian Hook**: "Everyone says 'follow up within 24 hours.' Here's why that's wrong."
5. **Personal Hook**: "I made a $50K mistake so you don't have to."

**Hook Criteria:**
- ‚úÖ Specific (numbers, timeframes, names)
- ‚úÖ Intriguing (creates information gap)
- ‚úÖ Relevant (to target audience's pain)
- ‚úÖ Promise value (worth continuing to read)

### The Body (Main Content)
**Purpose**: Deliver on hook's promise, provide value, build authority

**Structure Options:**

**Option 1: Problem-Solution-Results**
```

[Solution/approach taken]

**Option 2: Listicle**

   Context + actionable tip

2. [Second insight]
   Context + actionable tip

3. [Third insight]
   Context + actionable tip

Bottom line:
[Summary + takeaway]
```

**Option 3: Story Arc**
```

Setup:
[Context, stakes, challenge]

Conflict:
[What went wrong or obstacle faced]

Resolution:
[How it was solved]

Lesson:
[Broader principle/takeaway]

**Body Best Practices:**
- Use short paragraphs (1-3 lines each)
- Include white space for readability
- Bold key phrases (but sparingly)
- Use line breaks strategically
- Include 1-2 concrete examples
- Add 1 surprising or counterintuitive point

### The CTA (Call-to-Action)
**Purpose**: Give reader clear next step, drive business goals

**CTA Types:**

1. **Engagement CTA**: "What's your experience with lead scoring? Drop a comment."
2. **Connection CTA**: "Following for more B2B growth strategies."
3. **Resource CTA**: "Download our free lead scoring template (link in comments)"
4. **DM CTA**: "Want our full playbook? DM me 'LEADS'"
5. **Soft Pitch CTA**: "This is exactly what we help companies implement. Curious? Let's talk."

**CTA Rules:**
- One primary CTA per post
- Make it specific and actionable
- Lower friction = better response
- Tie to the value provided
- Test different types

### Hashtags
**Purpose**: Discoverability, topic clustering, professional positioning

**Strategy:**
- 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Mix of broad and niche
- Include 1 branded hashtag
- Position at end of post
- Use industry-standard tags

**Example Set:**

```

**Adaptation:**
- Break long paragraphs into individual tweets
- Add more personality/casual language
- Use thread numbers for readability

## Optimization Tips

### Algorithm Factors
LinkedIn prioritizes posts with:
- **Dwell Time**: Keep readers on your post longer
- **Early Engagement**: First 60 minutes critical
- **Comments > Likes**: Aim for conversation
- **Personal Posts > Company Posts**: Use personal profile
- **Native Content**: No external links in post body

### Engagement Tactics
1. **Ask Questions**: End with question to prompt comments
2. **Respond Fast**: Reply to comments within first hour
3. **Tag Strategically**: Mention relevant people (sparingly)
4. **Post Timing**: Test your audience (typically Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-1pm)
5. **Engagement Pods**: Share with group for initial boost
6. **Employee Amplification**: Have team engage early

### Testing Framework
Track these metrics:
- **Impressions**: How many saw it
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + comments + shares) / impressions
- **Click-through Rate**: If CTA includes action
- **Quality of Comments**: Depth of discussion
- **Follower Growth**: Net new from post
- **Lead Generation**: DMs or conversions attributed

Test variables:
- Hook styles (stat vs story vs question)
- Post length (short vs long)
- Time of day
- Day of week
- Topic categories
- CTA types
