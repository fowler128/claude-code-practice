## 4) Red flags

- ITAR/export-control constraints can sharply narrow foreign national placement in some energy/deep-tech roles.
- Large or parent-owned companies may already have panel firms, increasing procurement friction.
- Some organizations may have “no sponsorship” language for selected roles; verify by function before outreach.
- Very small teams (<20) may be founder-constrained and not ready for formal outside counsel.
