## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 Phase A: Pre-Engagement (Pre-Kickoff)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Conduct discovery call to confirm scope and fit
- Deliver pre-call snapshot questionnaire
- Validate engagement prerequisites

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Complete pre-call questionnaire
- Confirm decision-maker availability for readout
- Execute this SOW and submit initial payment

**Deliverable:** Signed SOW, scheduled kickoff

---

### 2.2 Phase B: Intake & Data Room Setup (Days 0-2)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Create secure client data room (Google Workspace)
- Provide standardized artifact checklist
- Send automated reminders for missing items
- Confirm data room completion

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Provide access to designated contact
- Submit required artifacts within 48 hours:
  - [ ] Org chart / role list
  - [ ] Tool stack inventory
  - [ ] 10-20 sample matters (redacted as needed)
  - [ ] SOPs and templates currently in use
  - [ ] KPI exports (leads, consults, retainers, cycle times)

**Deliverable:** Completed data room with all artifacts logged

---

### 2.3 Phase C: Readiness Assessment (Days 3-6)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Conduct process walkthroughs with designated staff
- Perform data quality profiling on submitted exports
- Complete systems inventory and integration mapping
- Score firm across 6 assessment pillars (0-5 scale):
  1. Process Maturity
  2. Data Quality
  3. Knowledge Systems
  4. Tooling & Integrations
  5. Risk & Governance
  6. Change Capacity

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Make available for interview (60-90 min each):
  - [ ] 1 intake coordinator or receptionist
  - [ ] 1-2 paralegals
  - [ ] 1 attorney (preferably managing partner or practice lead)
- Provide screen-share access to demonstrate current workflows

**Deliverable:** Completed assessment workbook (internal)

---

### 2.4 Phase D: Use-Case Portfolio & ROI Model (Days 7-9)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Identify candidate AI use cases from assessment findings
- Score use cases by feasibility, impact, and risk
- Build ROI model with assumptions documented
- Draft use-case one-pagers for top 3-5 opportunities

**Use-Case Selection Criteria:**
- High volume + repetitive + rules-based
- Clear success metrics (cycle time, rework, conversion)
- Low ambiguity or defined escalation rules
- Acceptable risk footprint with controls

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Validate volume and frequency assumptions
- Confirm strategic priorities for use-case ranking

**Deliverable:** Ranked use-case portfolio with ROI projections

---

### 2.5 Phase E: Roadmap & Governance Kit (Days 10-12)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Compile AI Readiness Scorecard (final report)
- Create Risk Register with controls and owners
- Build Prioritized Roadmap (30/60/90 days)
- Draft Data Cleanup Plan (taxonomy, naming, minimum viable data)
- Assemble Governance Starter Kit:
  - AI Acceptable Use Policy
  - Access/Permissions Model
  - Retention & Logging Requirements
  - Human-in-the-Loop Rules

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Review draft materials prior to final presentation
- Confirm attendees for executive readout

**Deliverable:** Complete deliverables package (PDF + source files)

---

### 2.6 Phase F: Executive Readout (Days 13-14)

**BizDeedz Responsibilities:**
- Conduct 90-minute executive presentation
- Walk through scorecard, roadmap, and governance kit
- Facilitate discussion on next steps
- Provide implementation proposal if requested

**Client Responsibilities:**
- Ensure decision-maker attendance
- Come prepared with questions and priorities

**Deliverable:** Recorded session (optional), decision on next phase
